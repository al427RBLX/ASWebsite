HICOM (High Command) Department Information

Job Description:
The Al427_RBLX HICOM Department is responsible for overseeing the studio's daily operations and work, verifying petitions or appointments from lower ranks, maintaining order and control, acting on behalf of the Al427_RBLX Studios Department, executing decisions impacting the studio as a whole, and effectively communicating with different departments across the studio to address issues altogether.

Game Admin Department Information

Job Description:
The Al427_RBLX Game Admin Department is responsible for the security, management, and order of all games of the Studio. They can work with the HICOM Department for related incidents and investigations requiring further research and authorization, and can enforce any of their powers as deemed necessary. Additionally, they can use moderation when a player breaks any of the rules and guidelines implemented in a game.
